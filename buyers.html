For Buyers

Discover and purchase premium digital content securely through Unblur.me’s Telegram-based platform.

Features

Preview Content

View blurred previews of locked content before making a purchase.

Simple Payments

Pay via PayPal.me with a memo to unlock content instantly.

Secure Access

Content is delivered within Telegram after payment verification.

Platform Security

JWT-based authentication, Firebase Firestore, and Cloudinary ensure secure data and file storage.

How It Works

  1. Click a shared Telegram link (e.g., /start view_[fileId]).
  2. View blurred previews of the content.
  3. Pay via PayPal.me, including the memo (e.g., [fileId]_[telegramId]).
  4. Receive unlocked content instantly in Telegram.
